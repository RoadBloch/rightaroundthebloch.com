About Ari Bloch

Ari Bloch is a high school senior on Long Island, NY.

His core subjects consist of calculus, chemistry, computer science, physics, and pre-calculus.

Ari is also very active within the extracuriculars offered at his school. He is co-president of the ping pong club as well as president, and former treasuer of the science club.

New this year, Ari along side of his friends founded a new club, Infinite Inquiry, which aims to teach other students about the more fascinating math that's taught beyond high school.

Ouside of school, Ari spends his time enjoying movies & TV shows, listening to music, coding, as well as learning about pure maths & physics.

More specifically, he is fascinated by different areas of quantum physics like quantum field theoryand neutrinos, astrophysics and black holes, quantum cosmology;

the observation of subatomic particles around black holes, topology, number theory, and calculus.
