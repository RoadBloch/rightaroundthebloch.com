About Ari Bloch

Ari Bloch is a college freshman originally from Long Island, NY. He is currently studying engineering mechanics-aerospace (EMA) at University of Wisconsin-Madison.

In his free time, Ari spends his time enjoying movies & TV shows, listening to music, coding, as well as learning about pure maths & physics.

More specifically, he is fascinated by different areas of quantum physics like quantum field theoryand neutrinos, astrophysics and black holes, quantum cosmology;

the observation of subatomic particles around black holes, topology, number theory, and calculus.
